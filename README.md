## üìà Key Metrics Explained

### Return on Investment (ROI)
**Formula:** (Net Profit / Total Costs) √ó 100
- **Above 100%** - Profitable investment
- **0-100%** - Breaking even or marginal profit
- **Below 0%** - Losing money, needs optimization

### Cost per Follower
**Formula:** Total Costs / Total Followers
- **Industry Average** - $0.50-$2.00 per follower
- **Excellent** - Below $0.50 per follower
- **Needs Improvement** - Above $3.00 per follower

### Revenue per Follower
**Formula:** Total Revenue / Total Followers
- **Excellent** - Above $1.00 per follower
- **Good** - $0.25-$1.00 per follower
- **Needs Work** - Below $0.25 per follower

### Conversion Rate
**Formula:** (Conversions / Website Traffic) √ó 100
- **Excellent** - Above 5%
- **Industry Average** - 2-5%
- **Needs Improvement** - Below 2%
